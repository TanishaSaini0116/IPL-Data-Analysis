This project analyzes 17 seasons of IPL cricket — from the very first match in 2008 to the 2024 final — using Python, Pandas, and Matplotlib.

Starting from raw Kaggle data, I cleaned over 260,000 ball-by-ball records, standardized team names across eras, and extracted six key insights about what really drives winning in T20 cricket.

The goal: find what actually predicts winning — patterns that repeat across every IPL champion.
